Nutritional Facts:

Here are the nutrition facts for 3.5 ounces (100 grams) of raw cashewnuts:

calories: 553
protein: 18.22 g
fat: 43.85 g
carbohydrate: 30.19 g
fiber: 3.30 g
sugar: 5.91 g

Most of the fats in cashews are monounsaturated fats.
The important vitamins and minerals in cashews include:

calcium: 37 mg
iron: 6.68 mg
magnesium: 292 mg
phosphorous: 593 mg
potassium: 660 mg
