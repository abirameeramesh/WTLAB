Nutritional Facts:

Here are the nutrition facts for 3.5 ounces (100 grams) of raw walnuts:

calories: 654
protein: 15.23 g
fat: 65.21 g
carbohydrate: 13.71 g
fiber: 6.7 g
sugar: 2.61 g
Walnuts have a slightly lower mineral content than other nuts:

calcium: 98 mg
iron: 2.91 mg
magnesium: 158 mg
phosphorous: 346 mg
potassium: 441 mg
